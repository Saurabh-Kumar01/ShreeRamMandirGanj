Shiv Mandir, Varanasi

Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India

About: Shiv Mandir in Varanasi is one of the oldest and most revered temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Pilgrims visit this sacred site throughout the year, especially during Shravan and Maha Shivratri.

Timings: 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily

Festivals: Maha Shivratri, Shravan Mondays, Kartik Purnima

How to Reach: Easily accessible by train, road, and air via Varanasi Junction and airport.
